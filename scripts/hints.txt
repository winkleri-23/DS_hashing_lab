DECODED SECURITY - HASHING LAB HINTS
=====================================

STEP 1: Look at the hashes
  cat /lab/hashes.txt

STEP 2: Generate your own MD5 hash to understand the format
  echo -n "password" | md5sum
  echo -n "password" | sha1sum

STEP 3: Crack MD5 hashes with John the Ripper
  john --format=raw-md5 --wordlist=/lab/wordlists/decoded_wordlist.txt /lab/hashes_md5.txt

STEP 4: See the cracked passwords
  john --format=raw-md5 --show /lab/hashes_md5.txt

STEP 5: Try cracking SHA-1 hashes
  john --format=raw-sha1 --wordlist=/lab/wordlists/decoded_wordlist.txt /lab/hashes_sha1.txt

STEP 6: Try cracking the bcrypt hash
  john --format=bcrypt --wordlist=/lab/wordlists/decoded_wordlist.txt /lab/hashes_bcrypt.txt

KEY QUESTIONS:
  - Which hashes cracked immediately?
  - Which hash did NOT crack? Why?
  - What is the difference between MD5 and bcrypt?
  - What would salting have changed?
